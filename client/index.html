WHAT IS GOLDEN VOICES

It’s all about the Community, The individual, the people. Anyone who has a story to tell.

At Golden Voices we believe in the power of community, we want to platform individuals to help them express themselves. Wether you’re a student ho wants to leave their mark on the city, or a inhabitant of Kortrijk who wants to share their story. Your opinion is everything to us.

What exactly will we do?

Inhabitant_

Subtitle

As a inhabitant of Kortrijk you have a very interesting view on the city. Your experience, needs and vision of the future are essential for creating a city that is appealing to everyone. At golden voices we want to give you the chance to participate in discussions, to give suggestions directly to the city and to make your voice matter.

Routes_

Subtitle

Starting from different points along the Leie you can follow a route that goes through beautiful and fun places in Kortrijk. This way you can discover the full charm of Kortrijk.
