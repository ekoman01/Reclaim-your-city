WHAT IS GOLDEN VOICES

It’s all about the community, the individual, the people. Anyone who has a story to tell.

At Golden Voices we believe in the power of community, we want to give individuals a platform to help them express themselves. Whether you’re a student who wants to leave their mark on the city, or an inhabitant of Kortrijk who wants to share their story. Your opinion is everything to us!

What exactly will we do?

Inhabitant_

Give you a chance

As an inhabitant of Kortrijk you have a very interesting view on the city. Your experience, needs and vision of the future are essential for creating a city that is appealing to everyone. At Golden Voices we want to give you the chance to participate in discussions, to give suggestions directly to the city and to make your voice matter.

Routes_

Discover Kortrijk

Starting from different points along the Leie, you can follow a route that goes through beautiful and fun places in Kortrijk. This way you can discover the full charm of the city.

When_

Routes and festival

You can follow these routes from September 2023 up to June 2024. Hereafter we will round out the year with a festival. There you can look at all the answers we collected during that school year.

But where can I find these routes?

The routes Pass through the center of Kortrijk. The converge at three points along the Leie.
