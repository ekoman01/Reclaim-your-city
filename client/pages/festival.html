The festival.

The perfect ending to the event. A festival to reflect on the year that has passed and to have some fun!

So........

  • Where?
  • When?
  • For who?

The Festival will highlight certain places in Kortrijk. The biggest place will be at the Broelkaai. Here a “golden wall” will be erected to show the data we collected over the past school year. The golden paths will also overflow with the golden vision of Kortrijk.

What can I do at the festival?

Walk

You can walk along one of our several routes spanning through the center of Kortrijk! On this walk you will pass along some of the nice places in Kortrijk where you can hang out with students and friends. This is everything from café’s, bars and fun little shops for ceramics, rugs, etc... But thats not all. At the end of every route we’ve chose some special places where a mysterious golden wall can be found.
